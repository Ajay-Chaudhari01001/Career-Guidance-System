List of Best Career Options after 10th Standard

11th and 12th PUC Courses after 10th

10+1 followed by 10+2 is the most followed path among students after 10th Standard. If you want to pursue a Bachelor’s Degree Course minimum eligibility is that you need to complete 10+2. That itself shows how important is 10+2. If you wish to follow this path there are options in front of you in terms of streams. Popular Streams available are

  • Arts stream
  • Commerce stream
  • Science stream

" Arts Stream "

Arts is the best stream for the students who wish to become Teacher, Lawyer, Anchor, Professor and want to go in Politics. In Arts, you will have several subjects like History, Geography, English,  Psychology, Economics, Political Science, Sociology, Sanskrit, and Philosophy.  Once you finish your 12th Arts Stream you can join any of the following

  • BA – Bachelor of Arts
  • MA – Master of Arts
  • B.ED – Bachelor of Education
  • M.ED – Master of Education
  • BSW – Bachelor of Social Work
  • MSW – Master of Social Work
  • M.Phil – Master of Philosophy
  • P.hd – Doctor of Philosophy

" Commerce Stream "

Commerce Stream can be extremely great for those who wanted to become Chartered Account, Professor, Bank PO, Teacher,  Finance Manager, Bank Manager, Stock Broker, Marketing Manager, Economist, Cost Accountant, Accountant Executive, HR, and many more. Various Subjects in Commerce Stream include Business Studies,  Statics, Economics, Mathematics, English, Computer, Accountancy,  etc.

After finishing your 12th commerce stream you can go for any of the following courses

  • B.com – Bachelor of Commerce
  • M.com – Master of Commerce
  • BBA – Bachelor of business administration
  • BCA – Bachelor of Computer Application
  • BMS – Bachelor of Management Studies
  • MBA – Master of Business Administration
  • Bachelor in Economics
  • CA – Chartered Accountant
  • CS – Company Secretary And many more.

" Science Stream "

Science Stream is one of the best choices that you can make from the list of best courses after 10th standard. It can be great for those who want to become Engineers, Doctor, Scientist, Pilot, Teacher, Professor, Chemist, Nurse, Loco Pilot and wants to join the Indian Navy, Indian Air Force, and many more.

You will have two groups in Science Stream namely

  • Group A – Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry
  • Group B – Biology, Physics, Chemistry

If you Mathematics you can go with any of the following courses after 12th

  • BE ( Bachelor of Engineering ) / B.tech ( Bachelor of Technician )
  • ME ( Master of Engineering ) / M.tech ( Master of Technician )
  • B.Sc – Bachelor of Science
  • M.Sc – Master of Science
  • B.Ed-Bachelor of Education
  • M.Ed-Master of EducationP.hd-Doctor of Philosophy

If you have Biology you can join the below courses after completing 12th

  • MBBS – Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery
  • BAMS – Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery
  • BDS – Bachelor of Dental Surgery
  • B.Pharm – Bachelor of Pharmacy
  • M.Pharm – Master of Pharmacy
  • Nursing
  • Veterinary Science
  • B.Sc – Bachelor of Science
  • M.Sc – Master of Science


Once, you finish the 10+2 you can choose a professional course according to your stream. Clear all your ambiguities regarding course details and opt from the plethora of courses open for you.

Things to Consider while Choosing your Career after 10th

Choosing your future should begin with Self Analysis instead of opinions from others. Don’t limit your choices with traditional thinking and look for tips to remember while choosing your career.

  • Think of your own priorities.
  • Have an Open Mind so that you can assess alternatives.
  • Do a SWOT Analysis(Strength, Weakness, Opportunity, Threat, Analysis) or go for a Career Advisor to help you out.
  • Ask your queries from Persons who are already into the Profession.
  • Don’t let anybody cause you to doubt your talents and abilities.
  • Focusing on your own interests will help you enhance your academic performance.
  • Do a Proper Research on every possible path available right in front of you.
